Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Overview

This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds on HomeListingAI subscriptions and services. We strive to provide excellent service and value to our customers.

2. Free Trial

No Risk Trial Period

  • All new users receive a 7-day free trial
  • No credit card required during the trial period
  • Full access to all platform features during trial
  • No obligation to continue after trial ends

3. Subscription Refunds

3.1 Monthly Subscriptions

No refunds are provided for partial months of service.

  • Subscriptions are billed monthly in advance
  • Service continues until the end of the current billing period
  • You may cancel at any time to stop future billing
  • Access continues until the end of the paid period

3.2 Annual Subscriptions

  • Annual subscriptions are billed yearly in advance
  • No refunds for partial years
  • Service continues until the end of the annual period

4. Refund Exceptions

We May Provide Refunds In These Cases:

  • Technical Issues: If our service is unavailable for more than 24 consecutive hours
  • Billing Errors: If you were charged incorrectly or multiple times
  • Service Cancellation: If we discontinue a service you depend on
  • Account Issues: If we cannot resolve account access problems

5. How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact Support: Email support@homelistingai.com
  2. Provide Details: Include your account email and reason for refund
  3. Review Process: We will review your request within 3 business days
  4. Decision: You will receive our decision via email

6. Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • PayPal refunds may take 3-5 business days to appear
  • You will receive a confirmation email when refund is processed

7. Cancellation vs. Refund

Important Distinction

  • Cancellation: Stops future billing but no refund for current period
  • Refund: Returns money for unused portion of current period (rare exceptions only)

8. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with our refund decision:

  1. Contact our support team for further review
  2. Provide additional documentation if needed
  3. We will conduct a thorough review within 5 business days
  4. Final decision will be communicated via email

9. Account Deletion After Refund

  • If a refund is approved, your account will be deactivated
  • All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • You will lose access to all platform features immediately
  • No data recovery is possible after account deletion

10. Contact Information

For refund requests or questions about this policy:

Email: support@homelistingai.com
Subject Line: "Refund Request - [Your Account Email]"
Response Time: Within 24 hours during business days

11. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. We will notify users of significant changes via email.
