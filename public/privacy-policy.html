1. Introduction

HomeListingAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered real estate management platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, business information
  • Profile Information: Profile photos, business details, license information
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely by PayPal)

2.2 Property and Business Data

  • Property Listings: Addresses, photos, descriptions, pricing, features
  • Lead Information: Contact details, preferences, interaction history
  • Communication Data: Emails, messages, call logs, meeting notes

2.3 Usage and Technical Data

  • Log Data: IP addresses, browser type, access times, pages viewed
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, mobile device identifiers
  • Analytics Data: Feature usage, interaction patterns, performance metrics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To provide and maintain our platform services
  • AI Processing: To generate content, responses, and recommendations
  • Communication: To send notifications, updates, and support messages
  • Analytics: To improve our services and user experience

  • Compliance: To meet legal obligations and enforce our terms

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services (email, payment processing, analytics)

  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to do so

5. Data Security

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure data centers with physical and digital protection
  • Employee access controls and training
  • Regular security updates and patches

6. Data Retention

6.1 Active Accounts

  • We retain your data while your account is active

6.2 Trial Accounts

Trial accounts and all associated data are automatically deleted after 7 days.

6.3 Deleted Accounts

  • Account data is deleted within 30 days of cancellation
