# HomeListingAI — AI Platform for Loan Officers and Real Estate Agents

## What It Is
HomeListingAI is an AI-powered platform that helps mortgage loan officers build agent partnerships and capture warm buyer leads — without cold calling.

## Primary Users
Mortgage loan officers who want a steady pipeline of pre-qualified buyer leads through real estate agent partnerships.

## Core Problem Solved
Loan officers struggle with two things: getting warm leads and staying top-of-mind with real estate agents. HomeListingAI solves both. The loan officer sends one link to an agent; the agent gets a live AI listing tool with the LO's branding; every buyer lead from that listing routes back to the LO automatically.

## Key Concepts
- **WOW Link**: A personalized invite the loan officer sends to a real estate agent. Contains a live demo of an AI-powered listing page with the LO's mortgage chatbot built in.
- **Share Kit**: Tools the agent gets once they publish a listing — tracked QR code, social post asset, open house flyer, sign rider PDF.
- **AI Buyer Chatbot**: Answers buyer financing questions 24/7 directly on the listing page. Pre-qualifies leads automatically.
- **Lead Routing**: All buyer leads from an agent's listing flow to the partnered loan officer.

## Automated Behaviors
- 24-hour reminder email if agent doesn't open the WOW link
- 72-hour reminder email if still no action
- Instant text + email to loan officer when agent claims their account
- Lead scoring and intent classification (hot/warm/cold)
